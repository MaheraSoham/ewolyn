# Reference Website: Enego

Main Website: [https://enego.co.in/](https://enego.co.in/)

## Key Sections & Features to Emulate:

1.  **Hero Section**: Focus on "Your Business Deserves Funding, Not Rejection". High-impact stats (approval time, funded amount, success rate).
2.  **Trust Bar**: Strategic Partners and Certifications.
3.  **MSME Success Stats**: Detailed metrics with Pan-India presence.
4.  **Government Schemes**: 
    - NAIFF Funding
    - NGO Elevation Program
    - SC/ST/OBC Schemes
    - Startup SEED Fund
5.  **Tailored Funding Options**:
    - Mudra Yojana
    - PMEGP
    - CGTMSE
    - PSB Loans in 59 Minutes
6.  **Process Steps**: 4-step process (Quick Application, Expert Consultation, Documentation, Approval).
7.  **Footer**: Comprehensive links, professional disclaimer, and specific payout instructions (trust-building).

## Design Aesthetic:
- Clean, professional, and corporate feel.
- High trust indicators (success rates, partner logos).
- Clear CTA (Check Your Eligibility).
